SoCreate Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect your data. Period. All your artwork, projects, crash reports, and personal data stay on your device. Google Sign-In connects you directly to Google — we never see your credentials. Crash reporting is opt-in and you own all your crash data.

1. Who We Are

SoCreate is developed by Steven Michael Allen Owens (@SoQuarky), operating under the umbrella company AdventuresInDrawing. Our brands include Soquarky, FunFYP, FPY.Lovely, and Soquarky.Click.

  • Website: soquarky.click
  • Support Email: support@soquarky.click
  • Crash Report Email: soquarky@artistso.com
  • Learning Platform: artistso.com
  • Privacy Policy URL: soquarky.click/privacy

2. Data We Collect

Short answer: We do not collect any personal data automatically.

2.1 Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Your artwork, drawings, and animation projects
  • Brush settings, custom brushes, and brush patterns
  • Layer configurations and layer effects
  • Canvas configurations and preferences
  • Undo/redo history
  • Auto-save backups and crash recovery snapshots
  • Timelapse recordings
  • Custom color palettes
  • Gesture preferences
  • Theme preferences
  • Audio recordings (voice tracks for animations)
  • Puppet mesh configurations and poses

2.2 Google Account Data

Your Google credentials are never shared with us. When you sign in with Google:
  • You connect directly to Google through Google's official sign-in system
  • We never see, store, or process your Google password
  • We never receive your Google authentication tokens
  • Your Google email is stored only on your device
  • Google Sign-In is used solely for YouTube video sharing and is completely optional

2.3 Crash Reports — You Own Your Data

All crash data belongs to you, the end user.
  • Crash reports are stored locally on your device only
  • Neither Steven Michael Allen Owens, AdventuresInDrawing, nor any brand under our umbrella collects, stores, or processes crash data
  • Automatic crash collection is opt-in (off by default)
  • You can view, edit, delete, and export your crash reports at any time

If you choose to share a crash report (completely optional):

  • You post the report to your own GitHub repository
  • You send an email to soquarky@artistso.com describing the issue, including:
    • Your device model and Android version
    • A description of the issue
    • A link to your GitHub issue
  • You can edit the report contents before sharing
  • We only see what you explicitly choose to send

2.4 Artistso.com Content

The app integrates with artistso.com to display tutorials, demos, and courses by @SoQuarky. When you browse content:

  • Content is loaded from artistso.com's public website
  • Standard web browsing data (as governed by artistso.com's own privacy policy) may apply
  • No personal data from SoCreate is shared with artistso.com

4. YouTube Sharing

You can share animations directly to YouTube through the app. This feature:

  • Requires Google Sign-In with YouTube upload permission
  • Works only with animations (not static images)
  • You configure the title, description, privacy level, and resolution before uploading
  • The default description includes links to SoCreate and artistso.com (you can edit or remove these)
  • Upload happens directly between your device and YouTube/Google servers
  • We do not see or store your YouTube uploads

5. Screen Overlay

The screen overlay permission (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW) allows SoCreate to display floating tools over other applications. This is used for:

  • Floating toolbar when drawing in other apps
  • Quick draw overlay for capturing ideas
  • Color picker overlay
  • Reference image viewer overlay
  • Animation timer overlay

This permission is optional and can be revoked at any time in Android Settings.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • YouTube uploads: When you explicitly choose to share an animation to YouTube, the video is uploaded directly from your device to YouTube/Google servers
  • Crash reports: When you explicitly choose to email a crash report, you send it from your own email account to soquarky@artistso.com
  • GitHub: When you choose to post a crash report, you post to your own GitHub repository using your own credentials

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Crash reports: Last 50 reports kept locally (configurable). You can delete them anytime
  • Auto-save backups: Last 5 backups rotated (configurable)
  • Email crash reports: We retain emailed crash reports only as long as needed to fix the reported issue, then delete them

8. Children's Privacy

SoCreate is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Google Sign-In is optional and governed by Google's own age requirements. Parents can contact us at support@soquarky.click regarding any concerns.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored on your device
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Export your crash reports
  • Revoke Google Sign-In access at any time
  • Revoke screen overlay permission at any time
  • Opt out of crash data collection
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device, protected by Android's security model. Google Sign-In uses OAuth 2.0, and authentication tokens are managed securely by Google Play Services. GitHub personal access tokens for crash reporting are stored locally using Android's encrypted storage.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at soquarky.click/privacy. Significant changes will be noted in the app via an in-app notification.
